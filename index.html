About the Quiz

The Tech Quiz, organised under Technova 2026, is a competitive technical quiz designed to test participants’ awareness, logic, and understanding of modern technology.

The quiz will include questions from computer fundamentals, programming, artificial intelligence, gadgets, and current technology trends. It aims to encourage logical thinking and technical curiosity among students.

  • Questions will vary from basic to advanced level
